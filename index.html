The Restaurant

Our Story

Nestled in Red Hook's industrial waterfront, Cafe Kestrel brings refined yet approachable New American dining to one of Brooklyn's most overlooked neighborhoods. Our space—converted from a historic warehouse—blends exposed brick, soaring ceilings, and warm copper accents.

Chef-owned and operated, we believe in the power of simple ingredients treated with respect. Our menu changes with the seasons, sourcing from farms we know by name. Whether you're celebrating or simply savoring a weeknight, Kestrel welcomes you to the table.
